# Landing Page

## Hero Section

### Headline:
**Parent Guidebook**

### Subheadline:
A free, bilingual guide to raising a whole, healthy, resilient child — written for parents, not doctors.

### Brief description:
Parent Guidebook covers four areas of raising a child: body, mind, daily life, and heart & soul. Every article is backed by published medical research, reviewed for accuracy, and written in language you can actually understand.

We started with children's eye health and are expanding to cover all major domains of child health and development.

---

## Explore by Area

Four categories, each with multiple domains:

- **For the Body** — Eyes (8 articles, live), Breathing, Bones & Movement, Teeth, Nutrition
- **For the Mind** — Attention & Digital World, Learning & Cognitive, Social & Emotional, School Life
- **For Daily Life** — Physical Activity, Outdoor Life, Home Environment
- **For the Heart & Soul** — Identity & Belonging, Gender & Confidence & Equality, Character & Inner Strength, Adaptability & Transitions, Big Questions, Explore & Future Readiness, How the World Works

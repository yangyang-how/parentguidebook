## Research Brief

Safety preparation during pregnancy prevents crises after birth. This article covers nursery safety setup, crib standards, car seat selection and installation, the baby-proofing checklist for before the baby arrives, and establishing the safe sleep environment. Getting these right before birth means fewer emergencies after.

## Red Flags & Nuances

- Co-sleeping is common in Chinese culture — safe sleep messaging must be clear about evidence while respectful of practice
- Product safety standards differ between countries — Chinese-made products sold in the US must meet US standards, but imported gifts may not
- Furniture anchoring awareness is lower in Chinese households — earthquake straps may be more familiar framing
- Temperature control in Chinese homes may involve different heating methods — adapt guidance

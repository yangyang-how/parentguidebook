## Research Brief

The first six months are dominated by safe sleep and supervision — SIDS is the leading cause of death in the 1-12 month age group. This article covers safe sleep practices in detail, SIDS prevention, bath safety, car seat installation and use, and fall prevention as babies begin to roll.

## Suggested Outline

- Key points: Back to sleep, firm flat surface, nothing in crib, room-share not bed-share, protective factors

## Red Flags & Nuances

- Bed-sharing is deeply cultural in Chinese families — the article must present evidence clearly while acknowledging practice and offering harm reduction
- 坐月子 caregivers (grandparents, 月嫂) must be included in safe sleep education — they may follow older practices
- In Chinese culture, warm clothing and thick blankets for infants are common — overheating risk needs addressing
- Car seat use is inconsistent in some Chinese communities — cultural education is needed
- The "prone sleeping helps with flat head" myth circulates in Chinese parent groups — directly address

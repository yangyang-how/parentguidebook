## Research Brief

The first six months postpartum are when community support matters most — and when it's hardest to access. New parent groups, lactation support, postpartum networks, cultural confinement practices, and isolation prevention are critical. This article covers finding and using support during the most vulnerable period.

## Red Flags & Nuances

- 坐月子 should be presented as a strength of Chinese culture, not something to be corrected
- Chinese immigrant families may lack the extended family support that 坐月子 traditionally provides
- Lactation support may be less accessible in Chinese communities — identify culturally appropriate resources
- WeChat groups for Chinese new parents are a major support resource
- Cultural stigma around seeking help may prevent Chinese parents from accessing support groups
